Find Out How Secure An Email Domain Is

Our app uses Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting, and Conformance (DMARC), Sender Policy Framework (SPF), as well as a brand impersonation check to generate scores for the security of an email domain. The lower the risk score, the safer the domain is. These scores help users to navigate safely through the email landscape and to avoid phishing attempts.
